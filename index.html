Steve Jobs

American entrepreneur, industrial designer, business magnate, media proprietor, and investor

  • 1977 - Release of the Apple II.

  • 1984 - On January 24, the presentation of the Macintosh, the first full-fledged powerful personal computer with a graphical interface controlled by a mouse, takes place. The beginning of the conquest of the computer market.
  • 1985 - Board of Directors removes Jobs from Apple. A trip to Europe, including Moscow. Romance with Tina Redse.
